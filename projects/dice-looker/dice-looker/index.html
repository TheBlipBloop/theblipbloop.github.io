The Dice Looker

26 August 2023

The Problem

I enjoy DnD. I own many dice. Sometimes, I want to show people all the cool dice I have. Sometimes people are “busy” or “at work” or “asleep”.

To fix this I decided to make a machine that could look at them with me!

The Solution

This theoretical dice looking machine would need to be able to A) recognize a dice and B) figure out where it is C) do those last two things in realtime. When it comes to looking at an image, figuring out whats in the image and then figuring out where those somethings are, no one beats YOLO (at time of writing). So we’ll be using YOLO for this project – specifically YOLOv8s.
