Ravenfield Multiplayer Project

21 July 2019

The Ravenfield Multiplayer Project (RFMP) was a community driven project to add a multiplayer (PvP) mode to the singleplayer game Ravenfield. I led a small team of developers in its creation starting in 2018 up to its release in 2019.

The project is available on steam as a free add-on to the base game.
