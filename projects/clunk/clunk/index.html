  • game

  • unity

Overview

Clunk is a simple PvPvE top down multiplayer survival shooter.

In this game players battle against an endless and ever growing horde of NPC enemies in a persistent world with day / night cycles. Players gain money by killing enemies (player and NPC alike). Money could then be spent on ammunition, better weapons, and base building elements. Bases act as respawn points that let players construct barriers, healing items, and defensive turrets to help them survive.
