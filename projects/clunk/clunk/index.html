CLUNK

16 May 2018

Clunk is a simple PvPvE top down multiplayer survival shooter.

In this game players battle against an endless and ever growing horde of NPC enemies in a persistent world with day / night cycles. Players gain money by killing enemies (player and NPC alike). Money could then be spent on ammunition, better weapons, and base building elements. Bases would act as respawn points and let players construct barriers, healing items, and defensive turrets to help them survive.

I’m not sure how well the netcode holds up today as I believe Unity made some changes to their relay services a couple years back, but it was fun back in the day.
