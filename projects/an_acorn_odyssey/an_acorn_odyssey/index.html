An Acorn Odyssey

7 February, 2023

  • game
  • personal
  • unity
  • C#
  • team-idk

Overview

Play as an acorn sprouting into a tree! Guide the roots of an acorn to nutrients while avoiding hazards.

Placed first at the Tucson Global Game Jam 2023 Site. Created with a small team in one week. I led our team and acted as the primary programmer on the project.

Mechanics

In this game, the player takes on the role of a root as it travels through the ground. Players control the roots movement using the mouse. While traversing, the root drains nutrients from the acorn — run out of nutrients and its game over. Players must be mindful of the path they try to take. They must also protect the vulnerable tip of the root from worms and other obstacles as they traverse through levels. The game also features several powerups: Fast Speed (makes you go faster), drill (lets you destroy obstacles and kill worms), Shrinker (shrinks the radius of the root opening up new pathways).

The root itself, though dynamically growing, features accurate real-time collision detection that prioritizes collision detection on parts of the root immediately visible to the player – resulting in excellent performance.
