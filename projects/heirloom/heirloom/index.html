Heirloom

03 May 2023

Your grandmother, a once famed adventure, has passed away, leaving you her cabin located on a floating island in the sky.

In the time since her passing, her cabin has fallen into disrepair — gather her missing inventions and using them to restore the cabin to its former state!

Heirloom is a movement based 3D exploration game created with a small team for a Univerity class (ISTA 451).

Completed in 7 weeks.
