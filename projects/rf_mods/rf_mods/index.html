Overview

I used to be very active in the Ravenfield modding community. Early in the games life, there was no built in modding support. As a result, all mods were hand patched DLL files that were distributed by the community.

The (DLL) Mods

An archive of some of the mods published by the community can be found here.

I made the following DLL mods:

  • Rainbow colored soldiers
  • Zombie mode
  • Aimbot
  • Big Vehicles
  • Bloodblock
  • Custom Crosshairs
  • The Magic Eraser
  • MAYHEM
  • Orange Balls
  • Slow mo
  • Fast mo
  • Super Shotgun
  • Mini Vehicles
  • Instant Vehicle Spawns
  • Star Wars: Force Mod
  • Sticky Grenades
  • Fall Damage
  • AA-AA Giver
  • Better Blood FX
  • Airstrikes
  • Stat Randomizer (PROJECT SWAGGER)
  • Fall Damage & Parachutes
  • Orbital Strikes
  • Weapon Attachments
  • Teleportation
  • Tie Fighter Vehicle Mod
  • Time Dilation (Quicksilver)
