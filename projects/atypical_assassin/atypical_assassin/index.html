An Atypical Assassin

24 October, 2021

  • game
  • personal
  • unity
  • C#

Overview

Doug, the Company’s former chief assassin, has had a terrible plumbing accident and is now somewhat lacking in the limb department! Help Doug eliminate his targets and reclaim his former glory!

My submission to the University of Arizona Fall 2021 Game Jam. Created from scratch in 7 days!

Mechanics

In this game, the player plays as a physics based ragdoll. They must guide the ragdoll through levels using only the arm(s) of the ragdoll to move. The game features several obstacles and items which the player must avoid / collect respectively.
