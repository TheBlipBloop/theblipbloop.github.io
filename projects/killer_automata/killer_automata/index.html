Killer Automata

4 May, 2023

  • game
  • personal

Overview

Killer Automata is a fast paced 2D top-down shooter I created with four other teammates in University. We worked on the project for 7 weeks and presented the final version at a University Showcase to industry partners, members of the community, faculty, and students.

“You are TWO-BIT, the world’s first sentient arcade machine. You’ve had enough of the flashy lights and chirpy sounds that infest this arcade. It’s time to make your escape. You must fight your way out of the arcade, pushing past state of the art security systems, to see what lies outside of the arcade.”

– Project Tagline

I coordinated team efforts, programmed core features, developed visual effects, and provided a small amount of art over the course of this project.
