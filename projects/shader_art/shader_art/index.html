Introduction

Lately, I’ve found myself delving deeper into Graphics Programming. I’ve been most fascinated by SDF raytracers. There’s something deeply satisfying about transforming a set of functions into a piece of art.

This page contains a series of my GLSL fragment shaders in rough chronological order.

Future Work

In the future I’d like to investigate Mandelbrot and similar fractals. I’d also like to set up realtime previews for my shaders on this site using WebGL.

Procedural Ocean at Night

This is my most recent creation, a pretty pixelated ocean! The surface of the ocean is represented by a weighted planar cross section of an animated 3D Voronoi diagram. There is a slight amount of reflectance on the water from stars in the sky. Stars and planets are animated and glow with a soft bloom effect.
