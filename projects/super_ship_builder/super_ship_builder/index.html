Super Ship Builder

08 September 2019

A physics based game where you play as an astronaut with extremely limited limb control that is trying to build a spaceship.

In Space Ship Builder the player’s job is simple: build a spaceship to explore asteroids and find more parts to build a bigger spaceship to find more asteroids!
