About

I’m Ben. I enjoy making art, programming games, and solving intricate problems. I’ve been working with Unity since 2015, Unreal Engine since 2019, and Godot since 2024. I’m well versed in a range of programming languages across a variety of domains and hold a B.S. in Computer Science (Minor in Game Design & Development). I prefer lower level languages that provide me with more control over hardware.
